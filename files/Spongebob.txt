*SpongeBob SquarePants* is a beloved animated series following an endlessly optimistic sea sponge and his quirky friends in the underwater city of Bikini Bottom. Living in a pineapple with his pet snail, Gary, SpongeBob works as a dedicated fry cook at the Krusty Krab, enthusiastically flipping Krabby Patties. His hyperactive nature frequently annoys his grumpy neighbor, Squidward Tentacles, but perfectly matches the loyal, dim-witted personality of his best friend, Patrick Star. Through its clever blend of slapstick humor, iconic catchphrases, and boundless energy, the show has cemented its legacy as a timeless pop culture phenomenon for audiences of all ages.
